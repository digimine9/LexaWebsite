About Lexa Beta

Discover the story and mission behind the app transforming education.

Our Mission

Lexa Beta was built to modernize how teachers run online exams. Beyond just hosting tests and tasks, Lexa empowers educators to track student activity in real time — helping prevent cheating and maintain academic fairness.

Our Journey

Started in 2024 by the lexa Team, Lexa has grown from a group project into a tool used by classrooms accross Canada. With a focus on innovation, we continue to evolve based on user feedback.
